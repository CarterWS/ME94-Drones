* Alternate between mouse and controller inputs by going into EACH waypoint object and typing in either “mouse” or “controller”

* For mouse: 
	Click locations where you want the drone to pass through
	Press the escape key to undo a line
	Press the tab key to switch between waypoints
* For controller:
	Move joystick to steer each waypoint
	Press the A button to save the path up until that point
	Press the B button to delete back until the previous save point
